Hospital Management Module

This Odoo module provides a comprehensive solution for managing hospital operations, including patient records, doctors, diseases, diagnoses, and visits.

Main Features

  • Patient registration and management
  • Doctor profiles and specialties
  • Recording and tracking visits
  • Diagnosis and disease catalog
  • Full visit and diagnosis history for each patient
  • Support for hierarchical disease categories
  • Role-based access and security

Usage

  • Register patients and assign personal doctors
  • Schedule and manage visits
  • Record diagnoses and track patient history
  • Analyze visits and diagnoses through reports

Intended Audience

  • Hospitals and clinics
  • Medical administrators
  • Doctors and healthcare professionals
